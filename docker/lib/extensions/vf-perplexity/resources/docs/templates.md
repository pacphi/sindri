# Perplexity Research Templates

Ready-to-use templates for common research scenarios, optimized for UK context.

## Business & Market Research

### Competitive Analysis

```
Research: [Company/Product] competitive landscape in UK market

Context: [Your industry/sector]

Focus areas:
- Market share and positioning
- Key differentiators
- Pricing strategies
- Customer segments
- UK-specific market dynamics

Deliverable: Comparison table with top 5 competitors, backed by recent UK sources

Timeframe: Last 6 months
```

### Market Sizing

```
Calculate total addressable market (TAM) for [product/service] in UK

Parameters:
- Geographic scope: United Kingdom
- Customer segments: [define segments]
- Revenue model: [B2B/B2C/hybrid]
- Comparable markets for benchmarking

Deliverable: Market size estimate with methodology, assumptions, and UK data sources

Include: Growth projections, market trends, regulatory impact
```

### Financial Analysis

```
Analyze financial performance: [Company name] (UK-listed or operating in UK)

Metrics to research:
- Revenue growth (YoY, QoQ)
- Profitability margins
- Cash flow trends
- Debt levels
- Valuation multiples vs sector peers

Sources: UK financial reports, LSE data, FCA filings

Format: Executive summary with key financial highlights
```

## Usage Tips

1. **Replace placeholders**: [Text in brackets] with your specific details
2. **Adjust timeframes**: Modify recency requirements based on topic
3. **Add context**: More context = better results
4. **Specify format**: Request tables, bullets, or prose as needed
5. **UK focus**: All templates default to UK context—adjust if needed
6. **Verify sources**: Always review citation quality and credibility

## Customization Examples

### Simple Query

```
Template: What are the current mortgage rates from major UK banks?
```

### Medium Complexity

```
Template: Research + Context + Constraints + Format

Example:
Research UK fintech companies focused on payments
Context: B2B merchant services market
Constraints: Revenue > £10M, Series B or later funding
Format: Table with company, revenue, funding, key features
```

### Complex Research

```
Template: Full five-element prompt

Example:
Context: I'm a product manager at a Series B SaaS company in London
Task: Research current best practices for PLG (product-led growth) onboarding
Focus: Reduce time-to-activation, increase trial-to-paid conversion, UK/EU benchmarks
Keywords: PLG, onboarding, activation, conversion optimization, SaaS metrics
Deliverable: Report with 5-7 actionable recommendations, case studies from UK SaaS companies, benchmarks

Timeframe: Last 12 months
Sources: Prioritize UK SaaS blogs, product analytics platforms, UK startup case studies
```
